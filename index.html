Why chose My Books ?

Reading is about sharing but do you always remember who you shared your books with ? My Books helps you to manage your library. In a glance, you see who borrowed your books and who lent you some. You can also meet other reading buddies to discover other writters and other genres.

Join our nice community !

How to use My Books ?

  1. Create your library

    2. Enter the books you have and that you would like to share. It's very simple ! You only need to scan their barcodes.

  2. Find your reading buddies

    3. Share your library with your friends and family. Thanks to My Books, you can also get in touch with people with similar reading tastes.

  3. Share and borrow

    4. Share and borrow books from your reading buddies. Thanks to My Books, you are sure not to forget who you lent your books to !

  4. Get inspired

    5. You can follow other members and read their opinions about the books they read.
